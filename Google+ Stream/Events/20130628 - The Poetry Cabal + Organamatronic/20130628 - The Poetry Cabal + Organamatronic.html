The strange yet beautiful wonder which is poets and musicians making art together is happening again, June 28th upstairs at Red Rock Coffee in downtown Mountain View.

Organamatronic makes improvised music out of a bewildering variety of instruments and devices, creating entire civilizations of sound.

The Poetry Cabal is a collection of poets who, for months at a time, meet only in basement rooms of hidden mansions, but emerge occasionally to question existence and demand answers
