Bill McKibben and Billy Parish Talk #MoneyShift

Created by Mosaic on Nov 13, 2013, 9:30:15 PM • Public event

📅 Nov 26, 2013, 11:00:00 AM – Nov 26, 2013, 12:00:00 PM

Join Bill McKibben (Founder of 350.org) and Billy Parish (Co-Founder and President of Mosaic) in a live G+ Hangout On Air to discuss #MoneyShift. MoneyShift represents a new model of shifting our collective money from the fossil fuel economy to a sustainable one. Now is the time to divest from fossil fuels and invest in renewable energy!
